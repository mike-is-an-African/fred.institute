The FDP Triangle
Fractions, Decimals, Percentages — converting in every direction.

Fractions, decimals, and percentages are three different ways of writing the same value. You must be able to convert between all three instantly.

The Three Conversion Rules Fraction → Decimal: divide numerator ÷ denominator
Decimal → Percentage: multiply by 100
Percentage → Decimal: divide by 100
Decimal → Fraction: write over 10/100/1000, then simplify
Fraction → Percentage: convert to decimal first, then ×100
Key FDP equivalents to memorise:
½ = 0.5 = 50%
¼ = 0.25 = 25%
¾ = 0.75 = 75%
⅓ = 0.333... = 33.3%
⅕ = 0.2 = 20%
1/8 = 0.125 = 12.5%

Fraction to Decimal Divide the numerator by the denominator.

⅔ = 2 ÷ 3 = 0.666... = 0.6̄
Decimal to Percentage Multiply by 100 (move decimal point 2 places right).
0.375 × 100 = 37.5%

Decimal → Fraction & Percentage → Fraction

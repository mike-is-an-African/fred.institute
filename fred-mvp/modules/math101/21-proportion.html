If 5 pens cost $3.75, how much do 8 pens cost?
1 pen = $3.75÷5 = $0.75. 8 pens = 8×$0.75 = $6.00.
C4 · Proportion
Direct Proportion
As one increases, the other increases by the same factor.

Two quantities are in direct proportion if when one doubles, the other doubles. Their ratio stays constant.

Direct Proportion y = kx where k is the constant of proportionality.

If 3 kg of flour costs $4.50:
k = 4.50÷3 = $1.50 per kg
For 7 kg: y = 1.50 × 7 = $10.50
The Unitary Method Step 1: Find the value for 1 unit
Step 2: Multiply by required number

If 4 workers build 20 m in a day:
1 worker builds 5 m per day.
7 workers build 7×5 = 35 m per day.

Inverse Proportion
As one increases, the other decreases.
Inverse Proportion y = k/x. When one doubles, the other halves.

10 workers take 6 days to build a wall.
If more workers → fewer days.
k = 10 × 6 = 60 (constant)
15 workers: 60÷15 = 4 days
1
Spot inverse proportion
Workers & time: more workers = less time. Inverse!
2
Find k: k = x₁ × y₁
k = 10 × 6 = 60
3
Find new y: y = k ÷ x₂
y = 60 ÷ 15 = 4 days
6 machines pack 600 boxes per hour. At the same rate, how long would 10 machines take to pack 600 boxes?

Graphs of Proportion
Direct proportion graph: a straight line through the origin (0,0). The steeper the line, the larger the constant k.
Inverse proportion graph: a curved line (hyperbola) — never reaches the axes. As x increases, y decreases but never reaches 0.

Key Points
  • Direct: y=kx. Both increase together. Graph: straight line through origin.
  • Inverse: y=k/x. One up = other down. Graph: hyperbola curve.
  • Unitary method: find value for 1 unit, then multiply
  • Find k (direct): k = y÷x. Find k (inverse): k = x×y
  • Currency, recipes, workers — all proportion problems

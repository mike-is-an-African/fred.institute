Division — Key Vocabulary
Know the terms before the method.

Division is splitting a quantity into equal groups. 12 ÷ 4 = 3 means "split 12 into 4 equal groups — each group has 3."

DividendThe number being divided. In 12 ÷ 4 = 3, the dividend is 12.
DivisorThe number you are dividing BY. In 12 ÷ 4 = 3, the divisor is 4.
QuotientThe result (answer) of division. In 12 ÷ 4 = 3, the quotient is 3.
RemainderWhat is left over when a number doesn't divide exactly. 13 ÷ 4 = 3 remainder 1.
Division by ZERO is undefined — it is impossible. You can never divide by 0.
In 45 ÷ 6 = 7 remainder 3, what is the divisor?

Short & Long Division — Step by Step
Short Division (bus stop method) 1. Write dividend under the "bus stop" bracket
2. Divide the divisor into the first digit(s)
3. Write quotient above, carry remainder to next digit
4. Repeat until all digits are used

Example: 459 ÷ 3
4÷3=1r1. Bring down: 15÷3=5. 9÷3=3.
Answer: 153
Long DivisionUsed for larger divisors (2+ digits). Same principle but working is shown in full — divide, multiply, subtract, bring down. Repeat.

Remainders & Dividing Decimals
Expressing Remainders As a remainder: 17 ÷ 5 = 3 r 2
As a fraction: 17 ÷ 5 = 3 and 2/5
As a decimal: 17 ÷ 5 = 3.4 (continue dividing: 20÷5=4)
Dividing Decimals Method 1: multiply both by 10/100 to make divisor a whole number.
3.6 ÷ 0.4 → multiply both by 10 → 36 ÷ 4 = 9

Method 2: short division, adding decimal point and zeros as needed.
When dividing by a decimal, always convert the divisor to a whole number first.

Division Sign Rules
Same rules as multiplication.
Sign rules for division:
Positive ÷ Positive = Positive: 12 ÷ 3 = 4
Negative ÷ Negative = Positive: −12 ÷ (−3) = 4
Positive ÷ Negative = Negative: 12 ÷ (−3) = −4
Negative ÷ Positive = Negative: −12 ÷ 3 = −4
Same signs → positive result. Different signs → negative result. This applies to both multiplication AND division.

What is −48 ÷ (−6)?

Key Points
  • Dividend ÷ Divisor = Quotient (+ Remainder)
  • Short division: bus-stop method, carry remainders left to right
  • Remainders: express as whole number, fraction, or decimal
  • Decimal division: make divisor a whole number first (×10, ×100…)
  • Sign rules: same signs = positive, different signs = negative
  • Division by zero is undefined — always impossible!

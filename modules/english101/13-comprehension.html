Reading & Summarising
Core concepts and definitions.

Reading comprehension means understanding what you read.

Steps for comprehension:
1. Read the passage carefully — twice if needed
2. Identify the main idea (what is this mostly about?)
3. Look for supporting details (facts, examples, evidence)
4. Understand vocabulary in context (use surrounding words to guess meaning)
5. Summarise in your own words

Question types: factual (find the answer in the text), inferential (read between the lines), evaluative (give your opinion with evidence)

Subtraction — Key Vocabulary
Learn the terms before the method.

Subtraction finds the difference between two numbers. It tells us how much one quantity is greater or less than another.

MinuendThe number you subtract FROM. In 9 − 4 = 5, the minuend is 9.
SubtrahendThe number being subtracted. In 9 − 4 = 5, the subtrahend is 4.
DifferenceThe result of subtraction. In 9 − 4 = 5, the difference is 5.
Borrowing (Regrouping)When a digit in the top number is smaller than the one below it, you "borrow" 10 from the column to the left.
Subtraction is NOT commutative: 9 − 4 ≠ 4 − 9. Order matters!
In 82 − 35 = 47, what is the difference?

Column Subtraction — With Borrowing
The standard written method, step by step.
Steps for Column Subtraction 1. Line up digits by place value
2. Start from the RIGHTMOST column (units)
3. If top digit < bottom digit: borrow 10 from the left column
4. Reduce the left column by 1 (you borrowed from it)
5. Subtract and write the difference
6. Move left and repeat
Example: 503 − 247
   5 0 3
 − 2 4 7
 -------
   2 5 6
Units: 3 < 7 → borrow from tens. Tens=0, borrow from hundreds.
13 − 7 = 6. Tens: 9 − 4 = 5. Hundreds: 4 − 2 = 2.
Answer: 256

Subtracting Decimals & Negatives
Align decimals. Subtracting a negative = adding.
Subtracting DecimalsAlign decimal points. Fill gaps with zeros. Then use column subtraction.

Example: 8.3 − 4.67
  8.30
− 4.67
------
  3.63
Subtracting NegativesSubtracting a negative = ADDING: 5 − (−3) = 5 + 3 = 8
Think: two minus signs cancel each other out.
−4 − (−7) = −4 + 7 = 3
Two negatives together make a positive: − − = +

Mental Subtraction Strategies
Quick techniques for mental calculation.
1
Counting Up (Shopkeeper's Method)
83 − 47: count up from 47 to 83. 47→50 (add 3), 50→83 (add 33). Total added: 36. Answer: 36.
2
Rounding and Adjusting
64 − 29: round 29 to 30, subtract: 64−30=34, then add back 1: 34+1=35.
3
Partitioning the Subtrahend
87 − 34: 87−30=57, 57−4=53. Subtract tens then units separately.

Key Points
  • Difference = result of subtraction. Minuend − Subtrahend = Difference.
  • Column method: line up by place value, start right, borrow when needed
  • Decimals: align decimal points before subtracting
  • Subtracting a negative = adding: a − (−b) = a + b
  • Mental strategies: counting up, rounding & adjust, partitioning
  • Subtraction is NOT commutative and NOT associative

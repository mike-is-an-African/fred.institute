What is a Percentage?
Per cent means "out of 100".

The word "percent" comes from Latin per centum — meaning "out of 100". A percentage is a fraction with denominator 100, written using the % symbol.

Percentage Definition25% = 25 out of 100 = ²⁵⁄₁₀₀ = ¼ = 0.25
100% = the whole thing
50% = half
200% = double
Key Percentages to Know 10% = 1/10 = 0.1
25% = 1/4 = 0.25
50% = 1/2 = 0.5
75% = 3/4 = 0.75
1% = 1/100 = 0.01

Finding a Percentage of an Amount
Method 1: Multiply by Decimal Convert % to decimal (÷100), then multiply.
35% of 240 = 0.35 × 240 = 84
Method 2: Fraction Method Write % as fraction over 100, multiply.
35% of 240 = 35/100 × 240 = 84
Method 3: Build from 10% 10% of 240 = 24. 5% = 12. 35% = (3×24)+12 = 84

Percentage Increase & Decrease
Percentage Increase New value = Original × (1 + %/100)
Increase $200 by 15%: 200 × 1.15 = $230

OR: Find 15% of 200 = 30. Add: 200+30=230.
Percentage Decrease New value = Original × (1 − %/100)
Decrease $200 by 15%: 200 × 0.85 = $170
Percentage Change Formula % change = (Change ÷ Original) × 100
Price rises from $50 to $65: change=$15
% change = (15÷50)×100 = 30%

Expressing as a Percentage
Writing One Amount as % of Another Formula: (Part ÷ Whole) × 100

A student scores 36 out of 48:
(36 ÷ 48) × 100 = 0.75 × 100 = 75%
A class of 40 students, 28 passed. What percentage passed?

Key Points
  • Per cent = out of 100. 37% = 37/100 = 0.37
  • % of amount: convert to decimal × amount
  • Increase: × (1 + r/100). Decrease: × (1 − r/100)
  • % change = (change ÷ original) × 100
  • Express as %: (part ÷ whole) × 100
  • Reverse %: divide by multiplier (e.g. 80% → ÷0.8)
